About Us

Welcome to Maasai Mara SACCO

The MM SACCO was registered on 23rd September 2014, certificate number CS/13657, under the Ministry of Co-operative Development, and its offices are situated within the Maasai Mara University main campus.

Our History

From Small Steps to Collective Strength

Maasai Mara SACCO Ltd was first registered as Maasai Mara University SACCO in 2011, with the objective of organising and promoting the welfare and economic interests of its members in line with co-operative values and principles.

Domiciled within the Maasai Mara University premises, the SACCO was formed to provide member-driven financial services within its common bond — after members found that outside providers could not offer services well customised to their needs.

From those humble beginnings, the SACCO has grown to a membership of over 600 as at December 2023, with an asset base of Ksh 170 Million — a major achievement in just over a decade, driven by customised member products, visionary leadership and sound management.

  1. 2011 — Founded

    Registered as Maasai Mara University SACCO to serve the campus common bond.

  2. 23 Sept 2014 — Re-registered

    Certified as a co-operative society (CS/13657) under the Ministry of Co-operative Development.

  3. Dec 2023 — 600+ Members

    Grew to over 600 members with an asset base of Ksh 170 Million.

  4. Today — Member-First

    Delivering affordable loans, disciplined savings and dividends to the Mara community.
