SACCO Chief Executive Officer

We are seeking a strategic and visionary leader to drive growth and operational excellence at Maasai Mara SACCO Society Limited.

Type

Full-time / Permanent

Location

Maasai Mara University, Narok

Education

Master's (Business-related) + CPA (K)

Experience

5+ yrs in SACCO, 3+ yrs in management

Job Description

Maasai Mara SACCO Society Ltd is a non-deposit taking Sacco registered under the Co-operative Societies Act (Cap 490) of the Laws of Kenya, with current membership of approximately 400 members. The SACCO Chief Executive Officer is responsible for providing strategic leadership, overseeing daily operations, ensuring financial sustainability, marketing and driving growth in line with the SACCO's vision.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide strategic leadership and vision for the SACCO
  • Oversee daily operations and ensure financial sustainability
  • Ensure regulatory compliance with SASRA and other authorities
  • Drive marketing and business development initiatives
  • Manage and supervise staff for operational efficiency
  • Work closely with the Board of Directors on strategic planning
  • Enhance member value through improved products and services
  • Implement financial controls and risk management procedures
  • Foster stakeholder relationships and partnerships
  • Lead digital transformation and technology adoption

Minimum Qualifications

  • A master's degree in a business-related field
  • Must be a CPA (K)
  • Bachelor's degree in Finance / Accounting / Co-operative Management
  • Five (5) years of practical experience in a SACCO environment, with at least three (3) years managing SACCO FOSA operations
  • Digital & technological competence, proficient in SACCO operations software

What We Offer

A leadership role at a growing, member-owned cooperative with the mandate to shape strategy, digitise services and deliver measurable member value — based at Maasai Mara University, Narok.
